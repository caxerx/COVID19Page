How To Prevent COVID-19

As the COVID-19 has high infectious, there are few actions and recommendations that help you avoid the virus.

Wear a Mask

Since the COVID-19 is spread by dropletes. Wearing a mask can barrier the virus dropletes and keep you safe in the crowded area.

Washing your hands and use hand sanitizer regularly

Washing your hands with soap and water or using alcohol-based hand rub can kills viruses that may be on your hands.

Avoid touching face

Hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses. Once contaminated, hands can transfer the virus to your eyes, nose or mouth. From there, the virus can enter your body and can make you sick.

Maintain social distancing

Maintain at least 1 metre (6 feet) distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

When someone coughs or sneezes they spray small liquid droplets from their nose or mouth which may contain virus. If you are too close, you can breathe in the droplets, including the COVID-19 virus if the person coughing has the disease.
